This is how our story begins

In 2010, readeo founder coby neuenschwander and his family moved 1,500 miles away-and quicky realized video calls alone weren't enough to keep his son and his son's grandparents connected.combining beautifully digitized books with video chat technology,readeo launched later that year.since then,reado has been committed to fostering the joy of reading between loved ones-near and far.

Thousands of books for the whole family

Bookchat gives families a way to spend quality time together,even when they're apart.we are passionate about growing our library with high-quality books that educate,entertain, and encourage litte ones to let their imagination run wild.

Turn screen time into story time

Bookchat lets either side of the video call flip through the pages of a picture book easily.this interactive experience makes reading together a joy for families everywhere.
