Introducing the Guest Reader Program

Our Free program combines video chat technology with interactive digitized books to help volunteers read to children anytime, from anywhere.

How will you use the program?

I'm a parent/guardian

Wanting to share this program with my child's class.

I'm a teacher

Excited to use this program in my classroom.

I'm an administartor

Seeking further information for my school or district.

Getting started is easy as 1-2-read!

Step 1

Set up your FREE account

Sign up using the teacher code located on the guest reader flyer.

Step 2

Choose a timeslot

Teachers set the schedule,you pick the available slot.

Step 3

Enjoy story time together!

Read, answer questions, and chat with the class.
